Google Data Analytics Certificate

Review

  • Mar 20, 2022

This is a very easy introductory level course for an aspiring google analyst, but nothing in depth. It basically provides you a taster session for SQL, Tableau and R but I do not think anyone would be ready for actual job with these tools. I was familiar with excel and R but it was still interesting to learn about SQL. I also liked how they courses were designed based on the steps that a data analyst would take for their tasks: ask/prepare/process/analyze/share. I took it as a easy side project while I was busy with other things in life.
